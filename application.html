Welcome to Tile Match Game

Overview

Tile Match is a fun and engaging puzzle game where you need to match 3 identical tiles to clear them. Some tiles are locked beneath others, and you must free them by clearing the ones on top first. How to Play

To play Tile Match, simply tap on a tile to move it to your selection space. When you collect three matching tiles, they disappear, creating more room. Be careful! If all 7 slots are filled with different tiles, you lose. Use strategy to clear the board and win the game!
