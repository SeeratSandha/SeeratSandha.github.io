How to Play

Step 1: Selecting Tiles

Tap on a tile to move it into your selection space. You can hold up to 7 tiles at a time, so choose wisely!

Step 2: Making Matches

Collect three identical tiles to make them disappear. Plan ahead to avoid filling all 7 slots!

Step 3: Unlocking Hidden Tiles

Clear the top layers to reveal hidden tiles underneath.

Step 4: Winning the Game

Clear all tiles on the board without filling your slots. As levels progress, the puzzles become trickier!
