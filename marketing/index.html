About Us

Since 1994, Warm Hugs has been a family-owned boutique in downtown Greeley, Colorado. We specialize in handmade food mixes, unique gifts, and cozy home décor. Our products are crafted with love, offering a warm hug in every purchase.

Our Products

  • Gourmet Food Mixes: Soups, breads, cookies, and more, made with all-natural ingredients.
  • Fashion & Accessories: Jewelry, handbags, and apparel from brands like Brighton and Judy Blue.
  • Home Décor: Candles, garden items, and bath & body products from local artisans.
